Quick legend:
  • Par: Par of the hole (3, 4, or 5).
  • Score: Strokes taken on the hole.
  • Putts: Number of strokes once on the green (or chip-ins putted from the fringe — your call, be consistent).
  • F (Fairway): Tick if your tee shot landed in the fairway. Only count on par 4s and par 5s (par 3s don't count).
  • GIR (Green in Regulation): Tick if you got on the green in regulation strokes. Par 3 = 1 shot, par 4 = 2, par 5 = 3.
  • UD (Up & Down): Tick if you missed the green BUT got down in 2 (one chip + one putt). Only relevant when GIR is not ticked.
  • Pen (Penalty): Number of penalty strokes on the hole (OB, water, lost ball, drop).
Best practice: Fill in after each hole if you can, or at the turn and end. Filling out hours later from memory loses too much detail.
