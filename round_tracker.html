Home course quick-fill

Tap to fill par, yardage, and course info. You can still edit anything.
Quick legend:
  • Par/Yds: Auto-filled from preset if you tap one. Otherwise enter manually.
  • Score: Strokes taken on the hole.
  • Putts: Number of strokes once on the green.
  • F (Fairway): Tick if your tee shot landed in the fairway. Par 4s and par 5s only.
  • GIR: Tick if you got on the green in regulation. Par 3 = 1 shot, par 4 = 2, par 5 = 3.
  • UD (Up & Down): Tick if you missed the green BUT got down in 2.
  • Pen: Number of penalty strokes on the hole.
Best practice: Fill in after each hole or at the turn/end. Filling out hours later loses too much detail.
