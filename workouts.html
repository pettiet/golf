Strength & Conditioning

Two 60-min workouts · Pick the day, do the work
When to do these: 2x per week, with at least one rest day between them. Recommended: Tuesday (Lower+Core) and Friday (Upper+Core). Done in the morning before practice, or on lighter practice days. Skip if your body is genuinely fatigued — golf reps come first.
Equipment: 2× 15-lb DBs · 2× 5-lb DBs · 2× 3-lb DBs · band gym (platform + bar) · ab wheel · 6-lb medicine ball
